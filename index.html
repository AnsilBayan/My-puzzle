RULES

* This is a 2 player game in which each player has 3 pieces and they have to move
their pieces through the lines in the square to make all 3 in the same line and whoever
does it first will win the game.

* You can control the game by both mouse and keyboard.

* You can play the game in multiplayer mode or against the engine. For first move of the game,
it is not allowed to place in '5'.

* For the hard level, You have to make each move within 10 seconds.

* If both the players together shuffled their pieces for more than 40 times and neither player
could win, it will be declared as DRAW.
